Parking Lot Wars

Parking Lot Wars is a casual, two-player racing game based off the experience of on-campus parking. The players play on one phone, and attempt to race to the open parking spot without getting damaged by either the player or events on the screen. The first to gain three suitcase items wins the game.

Features

Car Selection

This game features a car-select screen. The three cars that can be chosen all have different statistics. The sports car offers speed at the cost of poor handling. The SUV offers superb handling and can take more damage, at the cost of reduced speed. And lastly, the sedan is a balanced vehicle in terms of speed and manuverability.

Multiple Levels

The game offers a total of three playable levels. The levels are based off of the seasons, which means there is a winter level, summer level, and a fall/autumn level for the players to enjoy.

Final Thoughts

Parking Lot Wars had a phenomenal impact on how I approach developing video games. In addition to polishing my scripting skills, being a part of this project taught me valuable lessons on UI Design. The three of us diligently on this game, I could not be more proud of what we created. It is currently on a hiatus due to our current dedications.
