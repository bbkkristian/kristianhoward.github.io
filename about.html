Candid Details

I was born and raised in the Southern California area. Once I received my high-school diploma, I immediately ventured into the collegiate experience at California State University San Bernardino. I first applied to be a Television Marketing Major; but was not until I went to orientation that I found out that they had a program that I was actually interested in: Computer Systems--Game Development Option.

Throughout my college careerI have joined my good friends to create these three experiences. I put my heart into our projects, spending many late-nights trying to get my code to work. "Welcome to Video Game Development", I told myself as I sat at the computer sipping my warm-hot tea. But once I see the results working, it was all worth it in the end.
